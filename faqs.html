Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?

- A collaborative event for learning, building, and sharing experiences in software and hardware

- An opportunity to turn amazing ideas into real projects

What is the theme of this year?

- Crafting electronic musical inst-ruments

- Mastering cutting-edge topics like cyber security and embedded programming

Who can attend?

- Only students of Unitiversity of Illinois are eligible to attend

- All genders and majors are welcome!

What if I don't have a team?

- We'll help to pair up hackers at our kickoff event!

- You'll also have the chance to meet other hackers by joining our Discord server!

- There will be a channel for you to find teammates!

What proficency is recommended?

- Prior experience with software or hardware is not required.

- Workshops during the event will introduce you to programming languages and developing for hardware.

- One team member must have OpenLab access.

Disclaimer on OpenLab Access!

- Only the team member of the group with OpenLab access is able to go into the lab, not the ENTIRE group!

When will it be?

- The event will run from March 29th to March 31st, 2024.

Where will it take place?

- The event is held at UIUC Electrical and Computer Engineering Building

- The kickoff in the morning will be at Room 2017

- The Hackathon will be at Room 3002 (Banquet room)

Have some other questions?

- You can email us to any questions you still have.
